Farmer Training: A Concise Guide

Who This Is For

Smallholder farmers, women and youth farmers, and farmer groups looking for sustainable, low-cost, climate-resilient farming solutions.

Training Topics

  • Soil health and fertility
  • Climate-resilient practices
  • Organic and agroecological farming
  • Pest and disease management
  • Water management and irrigation
  • Seed saving and biodiversity
  • Post-harvest handling
  • Market access and value addition

How the Training Works

Farmers learn through short video lessons in local languages. Content is mobile-friendly and designed for self-paced or group learning.

Learning Formats

  • Short video lessons
  • On-farm demonstration videos
  • Printable learning guides
  • Community knowledge exchange
  • Group learning sessions

Benefits for Farmers

  • Lower input costs
  • Healthier soil and crops
  • Improved yields over time
  • Greater climate resilience
  • Stronger local knowledge systems

Language & Accessibility

Training is available in local languages and designed for different literacy levels using visual and practical demonstrations.
